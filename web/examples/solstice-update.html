05 / 10
Macro context

Market outlook: selective optimism.

Tailwinds

Enterprise AI adoption is moving from POC to production budgets. Average enterprise AI spend grew 3.4× YoY — our infrastructure and vertical SaaS positions benefit directly.

Rate environment stabilising. Growth equity multiples recovering toward 2022 levels for companies with strong NRR and visible paths to profitability.

Secondary market liquidity improving — GP-led secondaries pricing at 88 cents on NAV vs. 72 cents in Q4 2023. Creates tactical partial-exit opportunities.

Headwinds

Regulatory uncertainty in EU AI Act implementation creates compliance overhead for our two regulated-industry positions. Monitoring closely.

Talent compression in AI engineering continues. Average time-to-hire for ML roles extended from 6 to 11 weeks across portfolio — friction on hiring plans.

Customer procurement cycles lengthening in financial services. Verdant Logistics impacted by supply-chain consolidation among top-3 customers (see slide 3).
