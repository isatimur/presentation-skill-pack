What is
RetroNet?

RetroNet is a federated developer community platform. You run your own node. Your data lives on your server. You connect to other nodes on the RetroNet mesh. Nobody owns the network. Nobody can sell your data. Nobody can deplatform you without your consent.

It's not nostalgia. It's a correct architectural choice that centralised platforms abandoned for profit reasons — and we're building it back.

WHAT IT
DOES.

core feature set · v0.9.4
01Node Mesh
Peer-to-peer federation across all RetroNet nodes. No central server. Content propagates on the mesh with cryptographic signatures.
● LIVE
02Portable Identity
Your identity is a keypair. Move between nodes without losing followers, history, or reputation. Your identity is yours — permanently.
● LIVE
03Open Moderation
Community-governed moderation layers. Every rule is public. Every decision is auditable. No shadow banning. No invisible strikes.
● LIVE
04Algorithm Marketplace
Write your own feed algorithm or install one from the community. Audit any algorithm. Switch instantly. Delete and revert.
▶ NEW
05Encrypted DMs
End-to-end encryption by default. No key escrow. Node operators cannot read messages. Metadata minimized.
