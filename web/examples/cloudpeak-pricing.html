Common Questions

Questions we hear every day

Honest answers — no sales-speak.

Can I change plans mid-month?

Yes. Plan upgrades take effect immediately (prorated to the day). Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. No cancellation fees — ever.

What happens if I go over my bandwidth limit?

By default we hard-cap usage at your plan limit and send you a warning at 80%. You can opt-in to overage at $0.02/GB — still well below AWS/GCP rates — or we can automatically upgrade your plan.

Are there egress or inter-region transfer fees?

No. Zero egress fees across all 14 regions for all plans. Traffic between your CloudPeak services is always free. One of the most common reasons teams switch to us.

How does Enterprise pricing work?

Enterprise is a custom annual contract based on compute, storage, and compliance requirements. Average contract size is $84K/year. We typically scope and sign within 2 weeks.

Is my data locked in?

Never. Cancel any time, and we'll export everything — databases, files, configs — in standard formats within 48 hours, at no cost. We also provide migration tooling to common alternatives.

Do you offer a free trial?

Starter is free to try for 30 days. Pro includes a 14-day trial with full access — no credit card required. Enterprise pilots include a 60-day proof-of-concept engagement.
