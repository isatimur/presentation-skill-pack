▸ THE PRODUCT ™   MALLSOFT v1.0

AMBIENT RADIO
FOR THE NOSTALGIC EAR.

WHAT IS MALLSOFT?
Mallsoft is a mood-first music discovery app built around atmosphere and place — not genre tags or popularity charts. You pick a scene (Food Court, Empty Carousel, Late Night Parking Lot, Hotel Lobby 1993), and Mallsoft's curation engine surfaces audio that dissolves you into that space. Fully licensed, fully discoverable.
DISCOVERY ENGINE — SCENESYNC™
Our proprietary SceneSync™ model was trained on 38,000 hand-tagged ambient tracks, environmental metadata (reverb signature, BPM variance, tonal warmth index), and 6 million listener listening-context signals. It finds the sound you didn't know you needed.
SCENES
220+ hand-curated sonic environments. Updated weekly by our editorial team of atmospheric archivists.
RADIO MODE
Continuous ambient stream. No algorithm interruptions. No "your daily mix is ready." Just the drift.
TAPE DECKS
Shareable mixtape objects with VHS-aesthetic covers. 84K tapes created in beta alone.
ARTIST PORTAL
Direct upload + scene tagging for indie ambient producers. 2,200 artists on waiting list.

▸ FEATURE 01 — SCENESYNC™ ENGINE

THE ROOM PICKS
THE MUSIC FOR YOU.

◈
ATMOSPHERIC TAGGING
Every track is tagged across 14 atmospheric dimensions — reverb depth, warmth index, nostalgia coefficient, tempo drift. Not genre. Feeling. SceneSync maps where you are to what you need.
◈
CONTEXT SENSING
Time of day, ambient sound (with permission), motion state, local weather. Mallsoft knows you're at a mall versus your kitchen versus a 3AM drive, and adjusts the sonic palette accordingly.
◈
DISCOVERY DRIFT
Unlike recommendation loops, SceneSync slowly mutates your soundscape across a session — introducing unexpected textures while maintaining the emotional thread. Surprise without disruption.
