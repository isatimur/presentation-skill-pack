The Problem

WRITING PLATFORMS ARE
EXTRACTIVE BY DESIGN.

Three structural failures bleeding independent writers dry.
01
THE PLATFORM TAX
10–30%
Substack, Ghost, Beehiiv — every platform levies a revenue cut on paid subscriptions. A writer earning $5K/month loses up to $1,500 before paying a single cent toward their own infrastructure. At scale, this compounds catastrophically.
02
RENTED AUDIENCES
0 emails
Writers don't own their subscriber lists on most social platforms. When Medium, Twitter, or a newsletter platform folds — or changes its terms — the writer loses everything. Your audience should live in your hands, not theirs.
03
BLOAT & DISTRACTION
47 tabs
The average independent writer uses 6+ separate tools: a writing app, an email platform, a payment processor, an analytics dashboard, a link-in-bio page, and a social scheduler. Cobbled stacks eat time writers don't have.
THE OPENING
52M people identify as independent writers. Only 2.3M earn any revenue from their work. The gap isn't talent — it's tools. Existing platforms were built to extract, not to empower.

Inkwell closes that gap.

Product · 01/03

THE EDITOR —
WHERE WRITING LIVES.

Built from scratch. No Quill.js hacks. No blocky content editors.
CANVAS
Inkwell Canvas is a distraction-free long-form editor that respects the shape of a writer's thought. No blocks. No formatting sidebars erupting mid-sentence. Just writing — with formatting that appears only when summoned.
→ Inline formatting with keyboard-first shortcuts — no toolbar interruptions
→ Focus mode: single paragraph highlight, ambient noise, word-count pacing
→ Version snapshots with prose diff — see exactly what changed between drafts
→ Offline-first with background sync — write on a plane, publish when you land
→ Smart footnotes, pull quotes, embedded asides — all rendered beautifully in email and web
DUAL RENDER
Write once — Inkwell renders to both beautiful email HTML and a canonical web archive. No copy-paste. No reformatting. One draft, two perfect outputs.
IMPORT ANYWHERE
Full migration from Substack, Ghost, Mailchimp, ConvertKit — subscribers, archives, custom domains — in under 10 minutes. We've tested every edge case.
AI ASSIST
Opt-in AI tools: headline variants, readability scoring, subject line A/B suggestions. Always in service of the writer — never generating content on their behalf.
