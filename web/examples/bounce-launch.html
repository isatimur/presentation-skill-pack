Other apps broke us. Here's how.

🪟

The Performance Trap

You don't post how you feel. You post the version of you that will get the most likes. That's exhausting.

📊

The Metrics Spiral

Checking like counts 40 times a day is not connection. It's a dopamine slot machine with your self-worth as the stakes.

🗑️

The Highlight Reel

Everyone's life looks perfect online. This makes your real life feel wrong. It isn't. The feed is lying to you.

✨

How Bounce fixes it.

1

Reactions, not posts

You react to what you see. No drafting, no editing, no caption anxiety. Just an honest moment captured in 3 seconds.

2

Zero public counts

Nobody sees how many reactions you got. No follower numbers on your profile. No leaderboard. Nothing to optimize for.

3

Everything expires

All content disappears after 24 hours. No permanent record. No archive to manage. Just the present moment.

4

Friends-only by default

Your reactions go to your actual friends. Not your followers. Not the algorithm. Not strangers. Real people who know you.
