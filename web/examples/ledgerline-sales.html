How it works

One API. Every money movement.

1
Embed the SDK (2 lines)

Drop our React component or REST endpoint into your existing checkout. No redirect, no iframe — your UX, our rails.

2
Funds captured & held in your ledger

We custody buyer funds in FDIC-insured accounts. Your platform ledger updates in real time. No bank partnership required on your end.

3
Trigger payouts via API

Call POST /v1/payouts whenever your logic says go — at settlement, milestone, or on-demand.

4
Seller receives funds in 90 seconds

RTP and Push-to-Debit rails deliver directly to any US bank or debit card. ACH fallback for remaining 4% of accounts.
