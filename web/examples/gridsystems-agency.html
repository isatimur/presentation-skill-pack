01 — The Problem

Brands fail before
they launch. Design is the reason.

68%
of funded startups rebrand within 24 months
at an average cost of $380,000 — avoidable with a proper system from day one.
  • 01
    Inconsistent brand expression
    Marketing, product, and sales each apply the brand differently. The result is a fragmented identity that erodes trust and commands lower prices.
  • 02
    Design debt accumulates silently
    Without a system, every new hire and contractor makes ad-hoc decisions. By year two, the codebase of visual assets is unmaintainable — and expensive to untangle.
  • 03
    Conversion left on the table
    A disorganised visual hierarchy confuses users at key decision points. Companies with cohesive brand systems convert at 2.3× the rate of those without. That gap is measurable and preventable.
  • 04
    Speed to market suffers
    When no design system exists, every campaign and product release requires rebuilding primitives from scratch — adding 4–6 weeks per release cycle.

03 — The Method 05 / 11

Five steps. One system. Zero ambiguity.

8–14 weeks end-to-end
01
Discovery &
Audit
Stakeholder interviews, competitive landscape mapping, and a full audit of existing brand assets. We document what works, what conflicts, and what is missing before any creative work begins.
Output Brand Audit Report + Competitive Matrix
02
Strategy &
Positioning
Define brand positioning, differentiation axis, audience segmentation, and messaging architecture. The strategic layer that drives every design decision downstream — and keeps the work grounded in business outcomes.
Output Brand Strategy Document + Voice Guidelines
03
Identity
Design
Visual identity creation: logotype, colour system, typographic scale, grid, iconography, and motion principles. Designed as a cohesive, extensible system — not a collection of isolated assets.
Output Visual Identity System (Figma + exports)

System
Build
Translation of identity into a living design system: component library, documentation, usage rules, do/don't guidelines, and template library covering the 20 most common production contexts.
Output Design System + Component Library
05
Activation &
Handoff

Output Brand Portal + Governance Guide + Metrics Review
