What we heard from you

Three challenges, one root cause.

T+1 reporting delay creates blind spots

Risk and treasury decisions depend on yesterday's data. By the time the morning report lands, market conditions have shifted — costing an estimated $8–12M annually in missed position adjustments.

Siloed data across 6 core systems

Loan origination, risk, compliance, and treasury each run separate reporting stacks. Cross-system analysis requires a 3-day manual reconciliation process — your words, from discovery.

Analyst dependency creates single points of failure

Your senior analysts — Sarah and Mark — are the only people who can produce the cross-system views leadership needs. Vacation, attrition, or scale = decision paralysis.
